Support

Help Center

Common questions about Budget Mint. Still stuck? Email us anytime.

When can I download the app? +

Budget Mint is coming soon on Google Play and the App Store. Check the homepage download section for updates, or email us if you’d like to be notified.

Is my data private? Does it work offline? +

Yes. Your data lives first on your device (local SQLite). Cloud backup via Firebase is optional — only if you sign in. Core tracking works offline. See our Privacy Policy.

How do Tour / group bill splits work? +

Create a tour, share an invite code, add shared expenses with equal or custom split, then use Settle Up to see who owes whom. You can also share receipts and invoices from a tour.

What languages are supported? +

English, Bangla, Hindi, and Urdu, plus light and dark themes.

What is Privacy Mode and biometric lock? +

Privacy Mode masks balances and amounts on screen (useful in public). Biometric lock uses your phone’s fingerprint or Face ID — we don’t collect your biometric data.

Can I export reports? +

Yes. Income/expense, parties, and cash/bank-style statements can be exported to PDF or Excel and shared — handy for archives or tax prep.

How do I contact support? +

Email support@budgetmint.app — tell us your device, app version (when available), and what went wrong. We’ll get back as soon as we can.

Still need help?

We’re happy to help with setup, tours, sync, or privacy questions.
