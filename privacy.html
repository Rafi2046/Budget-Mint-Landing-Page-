Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Budget Mint is a personal finance and group tour bill-split app. We designed it to keep your financial data under your control — local-first on your device, with optional cloud backup only if you choose to sign in.

Data Ownership

Your ledger, budgets, tours, and related records are stored primarily in a local database on your device (SQLite). You own this data. We do not require an account to use the core offline features of the app.

Firebase Backup (optional)

If you sign in and enable sync, a backup of your data may be stored in Firebase / Firestore solely for backup and multi-device sync. Cloud storage is opt-in. If you never sign in for sync, your finance data stays on your device.

Security

The app runs in the device sandbox. You can enable biometric lock (fingerprint / Face ID) to protect access to the app. Biometric credentials are handled by your device’s system — Budget Mint does not collect or store your fingerprint or face data.

No Selling or Sharing of Finance Data

We do not sell your financial data to advertisers or analytics networks. We do not share your ledger contents with third parties for marketing. Optional cloud sync is for your backup only.

Privacy Mode

Privacy Mode lets you mask balances and amounts on screen (for example with ***) so others nearby cannot easily read your figures. This is a local display preference and does not send masked or unmasked amounts to us beyond what optional sync already covers.

Deletion Rights

You can delete your local data and, if applicable, your cloud account / synced data from within the app Settings. After deletion, associated backups under your control are removed according to the deletion flow in the app.

Contact

Questions about privacy or your data: support@budgetmint.app
